<p><p>The livestock animal vaccines market comprises several types, including live attenuated vaccines, inactivated vaccines, and other vaccine variants. Live attenuated vaccines contain weakened forms of pathogens, eliciting strong immune responses without causing disease. Inactivated vaccines feature killed pathogens, ensuring safety but often requiring booster doses for effective immunity. Other vaccines may include subunit, conjugate, or DNA vaccines, employing various innovative technologies to enhance protection against livestock diseases. These options cater to diverse animal health needs and improve overall agricultural productivity.</p></p>
