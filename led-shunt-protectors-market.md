This is particularly important in applications where consistent lighting is essential, such as in commercial and industrial settings.</p><p>The LED Shunt Protectors Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing adoption of LED lighting solutions across various sectors, including residential, commercial, and automotive.
