<p><p>The InGaAs Avalanche Photodetector market is divided into two primary types: line-mode and Geiger-mode. Line-mode detectors are designed for continuous signal detection, providing high-speed and linear response for applications like telecommunications and imaging. In contrast, Geiger-mode detectors operate in a single-photon counting regime, ideal for low-light detection and achieving high sensitivity, making them suitable for applications in quantum communications and biomedical research. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance requirements and operational characteristics.</p></p>
