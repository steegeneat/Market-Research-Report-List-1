<p><p>The Raw Meat Fat Analysis Equipment Market comprises two primary technologies: DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) and NIR (Near-Infrared Spectroscopy). DEXA technology utilizes X-ray beams to measure fat content through differential absorption, ensuring precise fat quantification and composition analysis. In contrast, NIR technology employs light reflection to assess fat levels rapidly, making it suitable for on-site analysis. Both methods are vital for quality control in meat production, enabling accurate fat measurement for improved product formulation and consumer information.</p></p>
