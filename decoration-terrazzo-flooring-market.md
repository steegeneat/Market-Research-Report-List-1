<p><p>The decoration terrazzo flooring market primarily consists of two main types: inorganic terrazzo and epoxy terrazzo. Inorganic terrazzo utilizes cement-based mixtures combined with marble chips or glass for a classic, durable finish, making it ideal for high-traffic areas. Conversely, epoxy terrazzo employs a resin-based binder, offering flexibility in design and vibrant colors, suitable for commercial spaces. Both types are valued for their aesthetic appeal, durability, and low maintenance, catering to diverse interior design needs.</p></p>
