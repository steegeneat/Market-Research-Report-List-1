<p><ul><li>Sheetfed Offset Press</li><li>Web Offset Printing Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Planographic press markets primarily consist of sheetfed offset presses and web offset printing machines. Sheetfed offset presses are ideal for short to medium print runs, offering high-quality prints with flexibility in paper types and sizes. They are commonly used for commercial printing, packaging, and high-quality publications. In contrast, web offset printing machines are designed for large-scale production, efficiently handling rolls of paper for high-speed printing. This type is frequently utilized in newspapers, magazines, and high-volume direct mail campaigns.</p></p>
