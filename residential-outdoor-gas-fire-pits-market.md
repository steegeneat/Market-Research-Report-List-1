<p><ul><li>Propane</li><li>Natural Gas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential outdoor gas fire pits market primarily consists of two types: propane and natural gas. Propane fire pits utilize liquefied gas stored in portable tanks, offering flexibility in placement and mobility. They ignite quickly and provide high heat output, making them popular for outdoor gatherings. Natural gas fire pits, connected to home gas lines, provide a more permanent solution with continuous fuel supply, eliminating the need for refills. Both types enhance outdoor living spaces, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
