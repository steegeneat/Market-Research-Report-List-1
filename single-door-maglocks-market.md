<p><p>The single door maglocks market consists of two main types based on installation methods: surface-mounted and embedded installations. Surface-mounted maglocks are installed externally on the door frame, offering easy retrofitting and convenience. Embedded installations, however, involve integrating the locking mechanism within the door, providing a more seamless and aesthetic appearance. Each type caters to different security needs and aesthetic preferences, allowing users to choose based on installation complexity and desired visual impact while ensuring effective access control.</p></p>
