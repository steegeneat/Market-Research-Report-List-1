<p><p>The edible plant-based oils market is categorized into herbaceous oils and woody plant oils. Herbaceous oils are derived from non-woody plants, such as seeds and nuts, and are often characterized by lighter flavors and nutritional benefits, making them popular for culinary uses and health applications. In contrast, woody plant oils are extracted from trees and shrubs, offering robust flavors and higher stability, ideal for cooking, seasoning, and cosmetic uses. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences and applications.</p></p>
