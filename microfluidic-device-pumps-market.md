Electronic type microfluidic pumps utilize advanced technologies such as piezoelectric or magnetohydrodynamic methods for precise fluid control, offering benefits like accuracy and integration with digital systems. Traditional type pumps generally use mechanical mechanisms, such as syringe or peristaltic pumps, providing reliable performance for simpler applications. Each type serves specific needs within various industries, including pharmaceuticals, diagnostics, and biotechnology, catering to different operational requirements and efficiencies.</p></p>
