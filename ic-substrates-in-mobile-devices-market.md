FC BGA (Flip Chip Ball Grid Array) substrates ensure excellent thermal performance; FC CSP (Flip Chip Chip Scale Packages) provide low-profile packaging for advanced designs. Other types may include advanced materials and technologies tailored to specific device requirements.</p></p>
