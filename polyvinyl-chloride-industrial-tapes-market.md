<p><p>The Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) industrial tapes market is segmented into two main types: single-sided and double-sided tape. Single-sided tape features adhesive on one side, ideal for applications such as insulation, mounting, and sealing. It offers easy application and effective adhesion to various surfaces. In contrast, double-sided tape has adhesive on both sides, providing superior bonding capabilities for tasks like laminating, displays, and sign-making. Both tape types are widely utilized in construction, automotive, and packaging industries for their durability and versatility.</p></p>
