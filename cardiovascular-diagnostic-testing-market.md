<p><strong>The Cardiovascular Diagnostic Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lipoprotein Test</li><li>Angiotensin Test</li><li>Fibrinogen Test</li><li>Serum Uric Acid Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiovascular diagnostic testing market includes several specialized tests that assess heart health. Lipoprotein tests measure the types and levels of lipoproteins in the blood, which are indicators of cholesterol levels and cardiovascular risk. Angiotensin tests evaluate hormone levels that regulate blood pressure, aiding in hypertension diagnosis. Fibrinogen tests assess clotting factors, indicating thrombotic risk. Serum uric acid tests measure uric acid levels, which can influence vascular health and risk for cardiovascular disease. Each test plays a crucial role in risk assessment and management.</p></p>
