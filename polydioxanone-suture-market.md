<p><p>The Polydioxanone (PDS) suture market consists of two main types: monofilament and multifilament sutures. Monofilament sutures are made from a single strand of material, providing minimal tissue drag and reduced risk of infection, making them ideal for delicate procedures. In contrast, multifilament sutures are composed of multiple intertwined strands, offering superior strength and flexibility, which can enhance knot security. Both types are utilized in various surgical applications, depending on the specific needs of the procedure.</p></p>
