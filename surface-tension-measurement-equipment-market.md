<p><strong>The Surface Tension Measurement Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Surface Tension Measurement Equipment</li><li>Dynamic Surface Tension Measurement Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surface tension measurement equipment market is categorized into two primary types: static and dynamic surface tension measurement equipment. Static surface tension measurement tools determine the surface tension of liquids at rest, providing a fixed value at a specific temperature. In contrast, dynamic surface tension measurement equipment assesses changes in surface tension as a liquid interacts with air or other phases, offering insights into the behavior of surfactants and the kinetics of surface phenomena. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including pharmaceuticals and food processing.</p></p>
