<p><p>Tumor ablation encompasses various technologies used to destroy cancerous tissues. Radiofrequency (RF) ablation utilizes electrical energy to generate heat, effectively targeting and killing tumors. Microwave ablation relies on microwave energy to create high temperatures for tumor destruction. Cryoablation employs extreme cold to freeze and destroy abnormal cells. Additionally, other technologies may include laser ablation and irreversibly electroporating systems. Each method offers distinct advantages tailored to specific tumor types, locations, and patient conditions, enhancing treatment personalization and efficacy.</p></p>
