<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Axial Bone Densitometry Measurement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (DEXA)</li><li>Quantitative Computed Tomography (QCT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The axial bone densitometry measurement market comprises two primary types: Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (DEXA) and Quantitative Computed Tomography (QCT). DEXA is the most widely used method, employing low-dose X-rays to assess bone mineral density, primarily in the hip and spine. It provides quick and accurate measurements, making it essential for osteoporosis diagnosis. QCT, on the other hand, offers 3D imaging and evaluates volumetric bone density, allowing for a more detailed assessment of bone quality and architecture. Both methods are vital for monitoring bone health.</p></p>
