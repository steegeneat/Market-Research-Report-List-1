<p><strong>The Boats and Yachts Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Actual Cash Value</li><li>Agreed Amount Value</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Boats and Yachts Insurance market offers two primary types of coverage: Actual Cash Value (ACV) and Agreed Amount Value. ACV policies provide compensation based on the market value of the vessel at the time of loss, factoring in depreciation. In contrast, Agreed Amount Value policies establish a predetermined value for the vessel at the policy's inception, ensuring that the insured receives this agreed amount in the event of a total loss, without depreciation considerations.</p></p>
