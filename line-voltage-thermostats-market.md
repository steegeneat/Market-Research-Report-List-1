<p><ul><li>Single Pole Wiring</li><li>Double Pole Wiring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The line-voltage thermostat market primarily features two wiring types: single pole and double pole. Single pole wiring impacts one wire to control heating systems, making it simpler and more cost-effective. It's typically used in basic heating setups. In contrast, double pole wiring connects to two wires, allowing for better control of both heating and cooling systems, making it suitable for more complex installations. Each type has its own applications, advantages, and compatibility considerations in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
