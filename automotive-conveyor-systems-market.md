<p><strong>The Automotive Conveyor Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Overhead Conveyor Systems</li><li>Ground Conveyor Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive conveyor systems market is primarily divided into two types: overhead conveyor systems and ground conveyor systems. Overhead conveyor systems utilize space efficiently by suspending the conveyor above the production floor, allowing for seamless movement of vehicles and parts without occupying ground space. In contrast, ground conveyor systems operate at floor level, facilitating the transport of heavy components and assembly lines. Both types are essential for enhancing productivity and streamlining manufacturing processes in the automotive industry.</p></p>
