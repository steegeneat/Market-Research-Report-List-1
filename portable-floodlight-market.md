<p><p>The portable floodlight market includes various lamp types, each with distinct characteristics. LED lamps are energy-efficient, long-lasting, and provide bright illumination with low heat output, making them ideal for extended use. Halogen lamps offer bright, white light and are known for their excellent color rendering but consume more energy. Incandescent lamps produce warm light but have shorter lifespans and lower energy efficiency. Fluorescent lamps are energy-efficient and provide good illumination but may require warm-up time and are less portable due to fragility.</p></p>
