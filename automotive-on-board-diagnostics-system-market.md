<p><p>The Automotive On-Board Diagnostics (OBD) system market consists of three main types: hand-held scan tools, mobile device-based tools, and PC-based scan tools. Hand-held scan tools are portable devices that connect directly to a vehicle's OBD port, providing real-time diagnostics. Mobile device-based tools leverage smartphones or tablets for diagnostics, offering convenience and connectivity features. PC-based scan tools utilize desktop or laptop computers for comprehensive analysis and advanced diagnostic functions, catering to professional mechanics and automotive technicians seeking deeper insights into vehicle performance.</p></p>
