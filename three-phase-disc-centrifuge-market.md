<p><p>The three-phase disc centrifuge market primarily comprises two types: Liquid-Liquid-Solid and Liquid-Liquid. The Liquid-Liquid-Solid centrifuge is designed for the separation of three distinct phases: solid particles, and two immiscible liquids, facilitating processes like wastewater treatment and chemical recovery. In contrast, the Liquid-Liquid centrifuge focuses on separating two liquid phases with different densities, commonly utilized in industries such as petrochemicals and pharmaceuticals. Both types enhance separation efficiency and product purity in various applications.</p></p>
