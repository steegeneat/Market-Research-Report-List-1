<p><p>Power Line Communication (PLC) systems are categorized into two main types: Narrowband PLC and Broadband PLC. Narrowband PLC is designed for communication over long distances with lower data rates, primarily used for utility applications such as smart metering and home automation. In contrast, Broadband PLC offers higher data rates, enabling multimedia applications like internet access and streaming. Both types leverage existing power line infrastructure but serve different communication needs and market segments, contributing to the evolution of smart grid technologies.</p></p>
