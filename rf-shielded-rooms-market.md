<p><p>RF shielded rooms are designed to prevent electromagnetic interference and are categorized into three market types based on the operation of their doors: manual, pneumatic, and electric. Manual RF shielded rooms require human effort to open and close the doors, providing a simple and cost-effective solution. Pneumatic systems utilize air pressure for door movement, offering smooth operation and enhanced sealing. Electric RF shielded rooms utilize motorized systems for automated access, ensuring convenience and efficiency in high-tech environments. Each type serves specific needs based on functionality and user requirements.</p></p>
