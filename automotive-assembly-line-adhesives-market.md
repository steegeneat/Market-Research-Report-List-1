<p><strong>The Automotive Assembly Line Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gap Fillers</li><li>Structural Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive assembly line adhesives market is primarily categorized into gap fillers and structural adhesives. Gap fillers are designed to fill spaces between components, providing insulation and reducing vibrations, while ensuring proper alignment during assembly. Structural adhesives, on the other hand, offer high strength bonding necessary for load-bearing applications, enhancing durability and structural integrity of automotive parts. Both types play crucial roles in the manufacturing process, improving efficiency, reducing weight, and increasing overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
