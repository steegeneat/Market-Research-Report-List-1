<p><strong>The Stainless Steel Suture Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monofilament</li><li>Braided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless steel suture market is primarily categorized into monofilament and braided types. Monofilament sutures consist of a single strand, offering reduced tissue trauma and minimal bacterial harboring, making them ideal for precise closures. In contrast, braided sutures consist of multiple intertwined strands, providing greater tensile strength and flexibility, which can enhance knot security. Each type serves distinct surgical needs, with selection depending on the procedure, tissue type, and desired healing properties.</p></p>
