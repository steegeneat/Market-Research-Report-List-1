<p><strong>The Angle Measuring Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Angle Measuring Encoders</li><li>Incremental Angle Measuring Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The angle measuring encoder market is primarily divided into two types: absolute angle measuring encoders and incremental angle measuring encoders. Absolute encoders provide a unique position value for each angle, ensuring accurate and immediate position feedback even after power loss. In contrast, incremental encoders generate relative position changes, requiring a reference point on startup but offering high speed and resolution in tracking movement. Together, these technologies cater to diverse applications in automation, robotics, and industrial machinery.</p></p>
