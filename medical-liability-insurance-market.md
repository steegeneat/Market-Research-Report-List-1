<p><strong>The Medical Liability Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>D&O Insurance</li><li>E&O Insurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical liability insurance encompasses various market types, including Directors and Officers (D&O) insurance and Errors and Omissions (E&O) insurance. D&O insurance protects medical facility executives against claims of wrongful acts in their managerial capacity, covering legal fees and settlements. E&O insurance, on the other hand, safeguards healthcare professionals from claims arising from negligent actions or omissions in their professional services, including misdiagnosis or inadequate treatment. Together, these insurance types ensure comprehensive protection for medical entities and professionals.</p></p>
