<p><p>Phototherapy machines for neonatal jaundice are specialized medical devices that utilize specific light wavelengths to treat hyperbilirubinemia in newborns. This condition is characterized by an excess of bilirubin in the bloodstream, which can lead to serious complications if left untreated. These machines work by converting bilirubin into a water-soluble form that can be excreted through urine and stool, effectively reducing its levels in the infant's blood.</p><p>The Phototherapy Machine for Neonatal Jaundice Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.4% during the forecast period. <p><p>The neonatal jaundice phototherapy machine market consists of two primary types: single surface and double surface phototherapy types. Single surface phototherapy machines emit light from one side, targeting the baby from a single angle, making them suitable for mild cases of jaundice. In contrast, double surface phototherapy machines provide light exposure from both sides, enhancing treatment efficacy by ensuring more comprehensive coverage. This dual approach is particularly beneficial for severe jaundice cases, maximizing bilirubin breakdown and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
