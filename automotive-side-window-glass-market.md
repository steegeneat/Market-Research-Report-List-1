<p><strong>The Automotive Side Window Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tempered</li><li>Laminated</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive side window glass market is classified into three main types: tempered, laminated, and others. Tempered glass is heat-treated for enhanced strength and safety, shattering into small, less harmful pieces upon impact. Laminated glass consists of two layers bonded with an interlayer, offering superior protection against shattering and reducing UV exposure. The "others" category may include specialized glasses such as double-glazed or coated options, which prioritize insulation or specific aesthetic qualities, catering to diverse automotive needs and preferences.</p></p>
