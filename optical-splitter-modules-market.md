<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Optical splitter Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fused Biconic Tapered Splitters (FBT)</li><li>Planar Lightwave Circuit Splitters (PLC)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical splitter modules market is primarily segmented into three types: Fused Biconic Tapered (FBT) splitters, Planar Lightwave Circuit (PLC) splitters, and others. </p><p>FBT splitters use a fusion technique to divide optical signals, offering cost-effectiveness and compact design, but with limited wavelength range. PLC splitters utilize a planar lightwave circuit technology, providing better performance across various wavelengths and higher splitting ratios. The "Other" category includes advanced splitters like arrays and dielectric-based options, catering to specialized applications and innovations in fiber optics.</p></p>
