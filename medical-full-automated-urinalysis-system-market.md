<p><ul><li>Dry Chemical</li><li>Flow Cytometric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Full Automated Urinalysis System market is primarily segmented into two types: Dry Chemical and Flow Cytometric methods. Dry Chemical systems utilize reagent strips to chemically analyze urine samples, providing quick and reliable results for parameters like pH, glucose, and protein levels. In contrast, Flow Cytometric systems employ laser technology to analyze cellular components in urine, allowing for detailed examination of particulate matter and a comprehensive assessment of urinary health. Both methods enhance diagnostic accuracy and efficiency in clinical settings.</p></p>
