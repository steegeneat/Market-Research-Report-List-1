<p><strong>The N TYPE SiC Substrate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi Insulating</li><li>Electric Conduction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The N-Type SiC substrate market is divided into two primary categories: Semi-Insulating and Electric Conduction. Semi-Insulating substrates exhibit high resistivity, minimizing free carrier concentration, which is crucial in reducing electronic noise in high-frequency applications. In contrast, Electric Conduction substrates allow controlled movement of charge carriers, making them suitable for power electronics and high-temperature applications. Each type serves distinct technological requirements, catering to industries such as telecommunications, automotive, and renewable energy, emphasizing the versatility of SiC materials.</p></p>
